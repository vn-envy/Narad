Key Research Findings (1)

The job automation landscape in 2025–2026

🔒

Platform Hardening

  • LinkedIn caps Easy Apply at ~50/day
  • Workday uses Cloudflare Turnstile CAPTCHA
  • Indeed has DataDome bot protection
  • Greenhouse & Lever added rate-limiting
📚

Dominant Open-Source Tool

  • AIHawk (feder-cr/Jobs_Applier_AI_Agent_AIHawk)
  • 15K+ stars, actively maintained
  • Uses LLM + Playwright for auto-apply
  • Key anti-detection: rebrowser-playwright patched browsers

Key Research Findings (2)

Risk levels & architectural patterns

🔐

Safer Architectural Pattern

Job discovery + semi-automated form filling
(not full auto-submit without review)

  • Human-in-the-loop for submission
  • Auto-fill forms, manual final click
  • Respect rate limits & robots.txt
⚠️

Risk Level Spectrum

Job discovery onlyLow-Medium
Semi-automated fillingMedium
Full auto-apply (personal)High
Full auto-apply (scaled)Very High

LinkedIn ToS explicitly bans automated access

Project Scope

✅

In Scope

  • Scrape jobs from LinkedIn, Google, Amazon, Microsoft + company pages
  • Rule-based filtering & weighted scoring
  • Auto-customize resume/cover letter via templates
  • Auto-fill simple ATS forms
  • Status tracking dashboard
  • Daily scheduled runs (cron)
❌

Out of Scope (Phase 1)

  • ML-based resume scoring
  • GenAI full resume rewrite
  • Complex multi-stage applications
  • Interview scheduling
  • LinkedIn InMail automation
  • Network scraping (2nd-degree connections)
